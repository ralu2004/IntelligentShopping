Service

"How does it work?" Customer Interaction: Login via smartphone app or touch display. Product Scanning: Automatically scans and recognizes products placed in the cart. Real-Time Recommendations: AI suggests complementary products and offers based on shopping behavior. Price Calculation: Real-time total displayed, including discounts and offers. Payment: Seamless checkout with mobile payment options or at self-checkout kiosks.
